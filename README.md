# ROCKS_OF_DOOM
In this game you control the player with your mouse movement and you have to beat all 7 levels in order to get the meteor cup. You start with 3 lives if you lose all of them you lose the game and start all over at level 1. The player is a doctor and he needs the meteor cup so he can drink his coffee however he needs to avoid red apple and plum meteors in order to get the cup. Green apples help him by giving him more lives to survive longer. Your preformance will be recorded based on how long it takes you to beat each level. Good luck because the doctor needs his coffee
